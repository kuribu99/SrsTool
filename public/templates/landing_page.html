Problems in Requirement Specification

Requirements were frequently written in ambiguous sentences and inconsistent manner which confuses reader, and incomplete which fails to specify all the important and core requirements. (Bures et al. 2012)
Functional requirements were often left out from requirement specification (Azuma 2004)
$81 billion was wasted on cancelled project and $59 billion was incurred for project extensions. The main factor that causes projects to be cancelled was also due to incomplete requirements of product. (Clancy 1995)
Inaccurate requirement gathering is the root cause of 38% of failed projects. (Project Management Institute 2015)

Our Objectives

To design and develop a tool that utilize boilerplates to assists user in requirement elicitation and specification phases and improve the quality of generated requirement as well as to produce a correct, complete and consistent software requirement specification (SRS)
