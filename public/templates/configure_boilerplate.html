Q: What is a requirement boilerplate and how it generates requirement?

To generate your requirements from your specification in the previous modules (Access Control, Action Control, etc), requirement boilerplate will be used to map the values into requirements.


You may configure these boilerplates to generate the requirement that best suit your needs, such as prefixing terms or cutting off unnecessary terms.


The idea of requirement boilerplate is to use <angle brackets> to enclose a word, which is known as placeholders, and then later, fill in appropriate values for the placeholder
